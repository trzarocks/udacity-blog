The Cookers

Weber 26" Kettle One Touch Gold

It's 40% larger than the iconic 22, and comes with stainless steel grates. You can cook a lot of 'que on here.

Weber 22" Kettle One Touch Silver

This budget 22 might be missing the ash catcher, but it's a workhorse. You can often find these used for about $40 without too much effort. There's no reason you can't own a premium grill.

Weber Charcoal Go Anywhere

The Weber CGA holds up to 6 burgers while cooking direct and the legs flip up to keep the lid in place. It's a searing machine that can also cook indirect for small batches of bbq.
